Final Fantasy Tactics Advance Original Soundtrack

by
Hitoshi Sakamoto, Kaori Okoshi, Ayako Sasou, Nobuo Uematsu

Publication date
2003
Topics
Soundtrack, Final Fantasy, Final Fantasy Tactics, Video Game
Original soundtrack for Final Fantasy Tactics Advance, a grid-based strategy RPG for the Gameboy Advance, which released in 2003. The Original Soundtrack CD album comes with remastered audio from the original GBA audio (Disc 1, GAMEBOY ADVANCE Original Version), and also completely remade tracks (Disc 2, Full Sound Version), although with some omissions. It has been uploaded to archive.org due to it's rarity and the fact it has been out of print for more than a decade. This way, people will be able to enjoy the soundtrack that many came to love from Final Fantasy Tactics Advance in it's full glory.
